Habit100

A lightweight tool to help users create and maintain healthy habits

 Habit100 helps users create new habits through a simple UI. The application will require users to complete their habits each day or else lose their progress. After 100 days the habit is complete and disapears! Habit100 is built with React and uses an Express-Mongoose API. The app is still in development and not yet deployed. Please take a look at the code, feedback welcome!
